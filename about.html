Trueline Africa Limited is a licensed East African Authorised Economic Operator (AEO) that delivers dependable customs clearance and forwarding services across the region.

As a customer-centric transport and logistics provider, we offer a wide range of freight forwarding solutions for both imports and exports to and from East Africa, connecting the region to global markets.

Our expertise spans air, land, and sea freight, managing various types of shipments including less than container load (LCL), full container load (FCL), out-of-gauge, break bulk, and heavy lift — from virtually any origin to any destination with any carrier.
