Have Any Query?

01 What types of goods can be transported via road and rail freight? +

Road and rail freight services from Trueline Africa are versatile, capable of transporting a diverse range of goods. These services can accommodate different types of cargo, considering factors such as the goods' nature, distance, urgency, packaging material and cost. Examples include general cargo, perishable goods, hazardous materials, bulk goods, automobiles, and household goods for road freight, as well as bulk goods, intermodal containers, heavy machinery, oil and gas, wood products, and manufactured goods for rail freight.

02 How do you ensure the safety and security of goods during transit? +

Trueline Africa employs various measures to ensure the safety and security of goods during transit, including proper packaging and labeling, cargo securing, GPS tracking systems, seals and locks, security personnel, careful route planning, insurance coverage, and detailed cargo inspection and documentation.

03 Is it possible to ship perishable items via air freight? +

Yes, air freight is a suitable mode of transportation for perishable items, including food, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive goods. Our team is experienced in handling all necessary cold chain requirements and documentation for perishable shipments.

04 What are the benefits of using multimodal transportation services? +

Multimodal transportation services offer cost efficiency, risk mitigation, flexibility, and sustainability benefits for logistics and supply chain management. This approach can result in shorter transit times, reduced costs, and enhanced cargo security by combining various modes of transport such as air, sea, road, and rail in a single shipment.

05 How do you ensure timely delivery with multimodal transportation? +

Timely delivery with multimodal transportation is ensured through meticulous route planning, real-time tracking, and proactive communication with logistics partners. Technology, contingency planning, and streamlined transshipment processes further contribute to meeting delivery deadlines across various transportation modes.

06 Can I use different transport modes for a single shipment? +

Yes, a single shipment can utilise various transport modes such as road, rail, sea, and air — known as multimodal transportation. This approach offers efficient and cost-effective logistics solutions, often resulting in shorter transit times and reduced shipping costs. We handle all coordination between modes seamlessly.

07 Can I store my goods with Trueline Africa temporarily? +

Yes, Trueline Africa provides warehousing and storage solutions as part of its services. We offer both short-term and long-term storage options with full inventory management, security, and distribution capabilities.

08 Why choose Trueline Africa for my logistics needs? +

Trueline Africa has the ability to provide comprehensive and efficient shipping solutions across the globe, with local expertise and support in each region to ensure smooth and reliable service. As a licensed AEO (Authorised Economic Operator) with over 14 years of experience and offices in Uganda, South Sudan, and Kenya, we bring unmatched regional knowledge and a proven track record of excellence.
